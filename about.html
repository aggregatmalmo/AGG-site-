About

“Location is the game, and X marks the spot.”

A platform appears when needed and disappears when done. Moving between the open and the dark room, mapping coordinates. What remains is pressure and a line on the record.

It started as a phone tree and a weather check, bridge shadows, park edges, loading docks, concrete recesses that could survive a night of sound. People learned the map by walking it, only meeting points and a time.

What we built was temporary; memory wasn’t.

The system adapted, rooms borrowed, rooms built, rooms hidden in plain sight. The image stayed the same. The method stayed consistent:

Make a floor that works; keep the door human; let the sound decide the hour.

Inclusivity was not a tagline but a procedure, fair entry, clear boundaries, eyes on the edges so the center could breathe.

An archive formed: recordings passed around, mixes filed. RAFT001 became the first document, many hands, one raft; fragments kept under tension so the energy traveled.

AGGREGAT remains a moving room, sometimes permitted, sometimes not, sometimes announced, sometimes implied.

Time the pressure, and the crowd completes the sentence.

Temporary rooms, permanent memory. Coordinates, not addresses.
