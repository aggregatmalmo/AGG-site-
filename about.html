About

AGGREGAT — Events • Label • Mix Series

Deeply rooted in Malmö’s underground, AGGREGAT has grown into a curated system of open airs, club nights, and cross-disciplinary projects exploring raw sound, visual identity, and uncompromising energy.

In recent years, the platform has evolved further under the direction of artist and founder BXY Santiago, working closely with a fluid network of collaborators.

Grounded in DIY ethics, inclusivity, and underground pressure, AGGREGAT has partnered with clubs, collectives, and cultural institutions across Malmö to develop its independent voice.

2025 marks a new chapter as AGGREGAT expands into label territory with the debut various artists compilation, RAFT001, curated by BXY Santiago and Christoffer Polizonis (Bad Balance).
