About

AGGREGAT is a Malmö-based underground platform focused on events, label releases, and mix archives.

Founded by BXY Santiago together with friends, Aggregat has evolved into a curated system of open airs, club nights, and cross-disciplinary events exploring raw sound, visual identity, and uncompromising energy. In recent years the platform has been led by BXY, working with a fluid network of artists and collaborators. Rooted in DIY ethics and inclusivity, Aggregat develops an independent voice across Malmö.

2025 marks a new chapter with the debut various artists compilation RAFT001, curated by BXY Santiago and Christoffer Polizonis.
