About

AGGREGAT is a Malmö-based underground platform focused on events, label releases, and mix archives.

Originally founded by BXY Santiago together with friends, Aggregat has evolved into a curated system of open airs, club nights, and cross-disciplinary events exploring raw sound, visual identity, and uncompromising energy. Over the last couple of years, the platform has been led by BXY, working with a fluid network of artists and collaborators.

Aggregat has partnered with clubs, collectives, and cultural institutions across Malmö — developing an independent voice rooted in DIY ethics, inclusivity, and underground pressure.

In 2025, Aggregat expands into label territory with the release of its first various artists compilation, RAFT001, curated by BXY Santiago and Christoffer Pollizoni.

Events. Label. Mixseries. No ceilings. Just pressure.
